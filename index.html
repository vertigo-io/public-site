Your are a Chief Digital Officer

Speed up your digital transformation and grow your business with new robust applications for your customers or collaborators.

Vertigo is made for real world projects and products, it is built with security and performance in mind. It's main target is core business applications. You can rely on it and focus on your business and what will make you stronger.

Vertigo reconciles sturdiness and innovation.

Start quickly and grow fast

With Vertigo your team can start working on your project within minutes. They have everything they need to build your MVP. Because anything built with Vertigo is made to last you're good to go and add more and more features.
You're ready for production from Day 1.

High quality standards

Too much business applications doesn't meet the quality level users expect. Vertigo is well tested, uses and promotes defensive coding to ensure that everything is always working properly.

Purpose oriented

Vertigo is designed for core business application. Even if it's possible to built any kind of software with Vertigo, it's in that field that it shows all its power : bring you a reliable application to leverage your business challenges.

On Premise/SaaS/Cloud : your choice

Because we know that deploying an application in production depends on many factors Vertigo always offer you options to deal with your requirements.
You want to take advantage of public cloud solutions ? No problem
You want to ensure data-privacy or comply with security constraints ? No problem, everything we rely on can be self-hosted

What's built in !

Vertigo-Core

Vertigo-core is an ultra light framework that provides everything you need to configure, run and monitor an app

Vertigo-Extensions

The Vertigo Extensions are modules that provides high level and homogeneous APIs to solve common and sometimes complex tasks.

Vertigo-Connectors

The connectors are low level bridges to external products and libraries. They offer the native client

Chatbot-Factory

With the Chatbot-Factory designer app it's super easy to create a chatbot and extend it to help your users! Take the leap of conversational user interfaces.

Vertigo-Mars

The demo app to guide you through our tour. It's kind of a show flat for business appplications!

Vertigo-Studio

The vertigo studio is the tool we have created to help developers. It provide useful tools and insights to monitor your app and improve your code.

Vertigo-University

A collection of sample projects that covers the main aspects of an application. These samples are exercices to guide you step by step!

Vertigo-Starters

A set of skeleton projects and maven archetypes to help you start a new vertigo project within minutes

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you offer free support?

    If you need help the best thing to do is reach us on our discord server. Otherwise we welcome all pull requests and take care to fix the reported issues on Github.

  • Do you offer paid premium support?

    Of course, if you need a premium support contact us at team@vertigo.io

  • What's your release plan?

    We generally release two major versions each year. We always make sure that migration from one version to the next takes less than 2 days of work. Version update also comes with update of all dependencies to always keep your project at the top. If critical bugs are found we release minor versions to fix them.

  • I want to help, how do I do?

    No problem come share with us your ideas! The best place is the discord server, in here we can really interact and make Vertigo even better!
